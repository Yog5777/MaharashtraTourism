    About Maharajbagh Zoo

    The Maharajbagh Zoo is located in Panjabrao Deshmukh Krishi Vidyapeeth in the heart of Nagpur city. It is the central zoo of the city and also a place frequented by families, children, and visitors. This popular zoo has been built on the gardens of Bhonsle and Maratha rulers of Nagpur. It comes under the administration of Central Zoo Authority (CZA) of India and maintenance of Panjabrao Deshmukh Krishi Vidyapeeth (PKV) of Nagpur. The zoo is home to many animal varieties including lions, tigers, leopards, deer, peacocks, etc. You can still have a nice picnic at the zoo while being close to nature. For children, it is fascinating to see wildlife so closely. A garden here, termed as the Maharajbagh has been converted into a botanical garden with a rich collection of herbs, shrubs, ornamental and aromatic plants, etc.Maharajbagh zoo is a zoo located in the heart of city of Nagpur. This Maharaj bagh was built by the rulers of Bhonsle dynasty of Maharashtra. This Zoo in Nagpur was recently renovated and the newly added botanical garden houses some rare species of plants. Animals at the Maharajbagh zoo Nagpur include leopards, lion, tiger, peacock and monkeys. Due to its location, many schools arrange picnics and educational visits to this zoo. You will find many walking trails and picnic grounds within the park which makes it an ideal family destination.



    THE HISTORY:

    The Maharajbagh was set up in 1894 and was a hunting place for Raje Bhonsle. However, the place of pride, was transferred to Agriculture Department of CP and Berar State by the Britishers. At that time the Maharajbagh comprised of few cages with one or two wild animals. In 1969, the green spot in the centre of the city was handed over to Dr Panjabrao Deshmukh Krishi Vidyapith (PDKV), Akola. From there onwards, the Maharajbagh was looked after by the Associate Dean of Agriculture College of PDKV. The age old cages had turned dilapidated but the PDKV started renovation and beautification of the favourite place of kids, youths, and the aged in 1992.Today, the Maharaja of Gardens, boasts of possessing a variety of wild animals like tigers, leopards, bears, monkeys of different species, foxes, peacocks, parrots of paradise, crocodiles, emu, wild cats and hundreds of flower trees, thick bushes.     Maharajbagh Zoo, Nagpur Overview

    Maharajbagh Zoo is one of the must-visit tourist sites in Nagpur, Maharashtra. It is a place that is frequented by the visitors on a huge scale. People of all age groups, especially the kids and wildlife photographers flock to this place and spend a couple of hours among the wild animals. A casual visit to this place is not a bad deal.?


    It Is Known For:-

    1) Maharajbagh Zoo is a famous travel destination in Nagpur that provides a shelter to the variety of wild animals.
    2) One can witness the animals like the tiger, leopard, panther, elephant, bear, hippo, spotted deer, monkey etc.
    3) It also has a rich avifauna that includes the parrot, cockatoo, hornbill, partridge, lark, duck, raven, white pigeon, cuckoo, hawk, owl, and much more.
    4) It is visited by the wildlife photographers that can capture the animals through the lens of a camera.
    Best Time To Visit:-

    Maharajbagh Zoo is a huge crowd puller. It never fails to catch the attention of visitors and thus, witness a huge footfall throughout the year. Therefore, it can be visited during any season of the year. However, it is recommended that the visit should be made during October-March as the pleasant weather makes it feasible for all to see the animals.


    Reaching There:-

    With a wider connectivity of the roadways, it is convenient to reach the zoo by hiring the service of the local buses and taxis. The local transit plies in plentitude on the route of this travel attraction in Nagpur.
