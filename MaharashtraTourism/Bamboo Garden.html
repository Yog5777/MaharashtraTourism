    About Bamboo Garden

    Bamboo garden is located on Old by pass, Vita Bhatti Road, Laghuvetan Colony, Amravati. It is well connected via all means of transport. Badnera Junction Stationâ is the nearest railway station being 15 kms away. State transport buses commute everyday and local taxis and autos are also easily available from anywhere to reach the garden.


    History

    Bamboo garden is a unique type of garden developed by the forest department with a motto to educate the people about the importance of use of various species of bamboo. Everything in the garden is built using the bamboos. The garden has been developed on 49 acres of area added glory to the historic Amravati city. One would also find a climber bamboo sapling here which is dark brown, wiry, rough stem, like domestic electric wire. The garden comprises bamboo hut, bamboo bridges, bamboo cave, bamboo forest information centre, cactus garden, lotus garden, childrenâs park. Some species of bamboo are brought from various parts of the country and abroad and will help for research work. People can see Vanchetana Kendra, Nakshatra Van along with children's park in the garden. The garden is adorned by two beautiful lakes - Wadali lake and the Futka lake which add to the picturesque charm of the garden. The lakes also provide a bird watch point in all seasons.


    Attraction of Bamboo Gardern

    People ideally spend 1 to 2 hours in The Bamboo Garden learning about the various species of bamboo, touring in the bamboo forest, enjoying boating and for kids the rides in the amusement park are a major attraction. The best time to visit The Bamboo Garden is during the early morning hours or the late evening hours as the calm atmosphere adds to the beauty of the place. It is suggested to visit the garden around 9 AM in the morning or 4 PM in the evening to get sufficient time to venture in the creative and unique garden.


    One must visit The Bamboo Garden as it is the first 'bamboo garden' of India in Amravati City. The sight of bamboos and bamboos of different species all around is indeed fascinating. There are more than a score of different species saplings in beds or polythene bags in the nursery. The garden is ideal for nature lovers who want to spend some time in the nature's lap. The garden is safe to take family members along for leisure and recreation. The place has an amusement park for kids where they can enjoy the rides. One can also enjoy boating in the serene lake and the horse riding at the place. The provision of adventure sports, lots of greenery to enjoy, pagodas and sit-outs for resting and a canteen run by women which serve a very tasty local food renders The Bamboo Garden an excellent place to spend weekends or evening with family or friends. The Bamboo Garden remains closed on Monday. On other days of the week, the garden can be visited during timings 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM. The entry ticket is INR 20 per person. The ticket price for horse riding is Rs 30. To get a ride on Toy train, ticket price for kids is Rs 30 while for adults it is Rs 50. The ticket price for adventure rope walk is Rs 10 per person. There are some other games as well which can be enjoyed for free.
