    Wadali Talao Amravati

    Tucked amidst mesmerising nature; Wadali Talao is a popular reservoir sprawled on Chandur rail road at Amravati. It was built to supply fresh water to the residents of the city, now serves as a refreshing getaway to the dwellers and travelers of Amravati. The charm of this place over children is also not too less, because of the nearby zoo, whose amazing and cheerful inhabitants fascinate the kids. One can also delve into an exhilarating boating experience and other water sport activities in the region.


    Wadali Talao Amravati is an ideal place for a picnic outing with family and friends, as it is just 3 km away from the city and can easily be reached by local transport. The best season to visit this place is during monsoon and winters, from July to February, when the reservoir is lush with clean water and weather is also pleasant.
