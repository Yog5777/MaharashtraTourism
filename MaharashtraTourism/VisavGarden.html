    History

    Visava Garden is a fun packed garden located in Vishnu Nagar, Nanded, Maharashtra. This garden is located around 5. 2 kms awayfrom the city centre of Nanded.


    Visava Garden is the only big garden present in the city of Nanded. This is not one of those regular gardens but it has featuresof amusement park. The centre of attraction here is the jurassic park themed garden, which has a few dinosaurs statues made of concrete. This garden is laden with small as well as big rides for children, teenagers and family. The rides includes train ride, merry go round and many swings and slides are there as well. The Visava garden has jogging and walking tracks and big green play areas as well. Therefore, a lush green environment attracts locals on a regualr basis.


    Some Interesting things about visava garden

    Visava Garden is a must visit for those who love being in peace surrounded with greenery. The park remains a little crowded by children as there are big play areas beautifully bordered with trees, bushes and flowers . The locals are also frequented here in the evening hours for jogging, exercising and evening walking. The Garden has the basic facilitiy of bathroom for public. It also has wheel-chair accessibility for the old and physically disabled people. There are food stalls present inside as well as outside the garden which provides with evening snacks and fast food at an affordable price. People usually spend around 1-2 hours at the park. The garden is well maintained and is kept neat and clean. It is advised to visit this garden during evening hours from 5:00 P. M to 9:00 P. M.
