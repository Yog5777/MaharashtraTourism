    Ambazari Lake Nagpur

    Situated in Nagpur, along its southwest border is the Ambazari Lake. It is one of the largest among the 11 lakes in Nagpur City. This lake is the prime source of water that is supplied to the officials of the Government and some important people. This lake is the source of the Nag River. This being a prime important place in the Nagpur city, it is taken care of and maintained by the Municipal Corporation of Nagpur. Ambazari Lake, being one of the most important tourist attractions in Nagpur, has been made a much more memorable place to visit as now, you can enjoy a musical fountain, amusement games and different types of electric rides in the garden next to it. The Ambazari Lake also provides boating facilities and a specific designed path for walking. All these facilities provided at the Ambazari Lake, make it a must visit place now and has therefore become a major tourists attraction during a holiday.



    HISTORY OF THE AMBAZARI LAKE

    The Ambazari Lake was built in the year 1870, under Bhonsle rule. The primary purpose of the lake was to supply water to the government officials and eminent people of the city through clay pipes. For over 30 years, the Ambazari Lake served its purpose exceptionally well. However, due to the constant pollution in the city, use of the lake as a water source to the city has now ceased.

    The Ambazari Garden that is located beside the lake was established in 1958.As mentioned earlier, The Ambazari Lake water is now supplied to Government officials and important people.This is supplied with the help of clay pipes that was built in 1870 under the Bhosle rule. It was built with the intension of supplying water to the entire city of Nagpur. The name Ambazari is given to it, as you will be able to see that it is surrounded by mango trees. In the local language of Maharashtra, Marathi, Amba is the name given to mango.



    GETTING THERE

    Since the Ambazari Lake is situated on the western side of the Nagpur City, you can arrive at this destination with the help of any public transport. The Ambazari Lake is very popular place for sightseeing tour, therefore public transport can easily help you to reach this place.



    THINGS TO DO AND SEE IN AND AROUND

    Apart from enjoying the facilities of boating, games and rides, tourists can also visit a band of pubs and restaurants, one of them being the Residence bar and Restaurants. The city also has Lila Vista Shopping Center and Prushottam Bazaar, so that you can enjoy some shopping spree.



    Ambazari Garden at Ambazari Lake

    Spread over 18 acres of land, the Ambazari Garden is a much loved picturesque tourist spot. A huge model of a dinosaur greets you at the entrance of the garden. To add to the fun here, the garden also offers a giant air-filled lion and teddy bear. Other amenities here include a mini train, see-saws, thrilling rides, slides, colombus and other joyful rides.


    The highlight of the garden is no doubt the musical fountain. The fountain changes its momentum according to the rhythm of the continuous music, while the superb lighting effects add to the overall ambience. Musical fountain shows are held every week in the evenings on Saturdays and Sundays. With its dense climbers and woods, the garden provides a jubilant and refreshing experience. It is a sheer pleasure to walk through this garden and appreciate the plant kingdom that thrives here.

    How To Reach Ambazari lake

    The Ambazari Lake is located on the western side of the city of Nagpur and can be reached quickly using all means of public transport. Since this tourist site is extremely popular, transportation facilities are readily available. For those of you who are staying near the airport, the Ambazari Lake is just 4 kilometres away. The railway station is also only five kilometres away. From both these places, taxis and cabs can be easily booked.
