    About Tikujini Wadi

    Tikujini Wadi is a water park and an amusement park. The water park, spanning an area with Giant Slides, Wave Pool, Kiddies and Family Slides, Lazy River, Landscape Gardens and Aqua play Shop. The entire Theme Park has been designed and constructed along International safety standards and emphasis on giving tremendous entertainment value with safety for the entire family and other peer groups. A spectacular design of rides and attractions of the Water park will make your visit a memorable experience. The splash pools and the water slides for all the ages will make the actual family excitement a reality. Toddlers, children, parents and grandparents will realize that summer fun doesn't get any bigger or better than at TIKUJI-NI-WADITikuji Ni Wadi


    Tikuji Ni Wadi in Thane West

    This is one of the most popular amusement parks near Mumbai. Tikuji Ni Wadi is located at Thane West in Thane and has ample of fun activities for the whole family. The place is surrounded by a wadi or an orchard where a lot of chikoos grow.


    Tikujiniwadi Resort, Thane was founded and established by Late Shri Pravinchandra A Parekh over 30 years ago as a resort for those looking to spend some peaceful time away from the city. Over the years this place has bought respite for thousands of kids and adults alike who have come here to snatch some enjoyable moments from their otherwise hectic life. Such is the popularity of this place that this resort has been recognized as a Tourist attraction by the Tourism Department, Government of Maharashtra.


    Entertainment at Tikuji Ni Wadi, Thane

    One has to a pay a one time entry fee of Rs.400, which takes care of all the rides inside. The amusement park has over 21 rides like a rotating spin coaster which goes over the treetops, or the one of a kind UFO Cycle which one has to pedal along a 500 meters track in mid-air. A few of the other popular rides are the Spin Coaster, Climbing Cars, Worm Coaster, Animal Kingdom, Kids Fairy Ride, Space Ship, Family Train, Giant Wheel etc. The amusement park timings are between 11:00 Am To 7:00 Pm.


    Tikuji Ni Wadi Water Park, Thane has many fun filled water rides for adults and kids alike. There are separate pools for kids and adults and many different types of water slides. There is also a wave pool and a lazy river. The water is always kept clean and there are life guards all around to ensure that the safety of the patrons are never compromised. The water park timings are between 10:00 Am To 6:00 Pm.


    The entertainment park also has a resort area which has a few fully furnished air-conditioned cottages for those who want to stay longer in the park. The resort timings are between 09:00 Am To 7:00 Pm. The place is also used for hosting corporate events and meetings as well weddings and other functions.


    The park also has a cafe and a restaurant in its premises which serves delicious vegetarian food.



    So come with your friends and family and have the time of your life at this resort. For Tikuji Ni Wadi address, scroll up at the listing above.

    How to Get there?


    Bus:TMT bus Route Number 56 from Thane Station, MSRT from Western Suburbs (Borivali) to Manpada Village Junction and walk, BEST BUS route 700 or TMT route number 65 from Borivali to Thane. Get down at Manpada Village Junction and walk.


    Car:Take the Eastern Express Highway from Sion Circle. Pass the Mulund Check Naka towards Kapurwadi junction on to Borivali Ghodbunder Road. Turn left on the junction of Manpada village.


    The place is especially famous for visiting during the festival of Shivratri because it hosts a massive idol of Lord Shiva which is carved out of ice and stands proudly for all to see.


    Things to do at Tikuji-ni-Wadi Thane

    1. Trying amusement rides – Tikuji ni wadi offers many dry rides and activities within the premises. The park has rides like Dashing Car, Go Kart and Worm Coaster for full family fun. And you also have exclusive kid-friendly rides at Tikuji ni wadi amusement park. Climbing Car, Frog Ride and Pirate Boat are among them. Kids can also try fun games at the Play Station.


    2. Playing water games – You can enjoy with your family in the splash pools and water slides of Tikuji ni wadi water park. The Wave Pool, Lazy River and kid-friendly rides are among the different activities available in the water play area. Toddlers and kids can have a fun-filled day with their parents at the water park. You will also find a cafe here.


    3. Visiting Dinosaur World – Another attraction of Tikuji-ni-wadi is the small dinosaur park. It has life-sized animated structures of various pre-historic creatures like Spinosaurus, Tyrannosaurus Rex, Pteranodon and others.


    4. Browsing Tikuji ni wadi aquarium – Tikuji ni wadi has a small aquarium too, where the entire family can watch fishes living in their natural habitat, and know more about them.


    5. Eating at the treetop restaurant – Tikuji ni wadi has cafes and snack counters spread throughout the park. They serve vegetarian snacks and dishes from different cuisines. And you also get tea, coffee and soft drinks. The treetop restaurant there also hosts a buffet lunch.


    6. Staying in a cottage – If you want to enjoy a Tikuji ni wadi night stay, you can book the air-conditioned cottages available within the resort complex.


    Things to keep in mind while visiting Tikuji-ni-Wadi

    1.Have your identity proof handy.


    2.Follow the age restriction on the rides.


    3.Keep all your valuables in the changing room lockers.


    4.Wear synthetic/nylon clothes during the activities in the water park.


    5.Apply sunscreen, preferably waterproof, when enjoying in the open.


    6.Stay out of the water if you have water-borne allergies.


    7.People with health problems should avoid adventure rides.



    8.Swimming costumes are available for sale.


    9.Food and drinks from the outside aren’t permitted during the visit.


    10.The resort doesn’t have provisions for dinner; order from outside.
